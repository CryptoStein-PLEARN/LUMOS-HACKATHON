The player's goal is to gain knowledge, make the best decisions by choosing the best offers, and earn a higher income. The game has various NFTs that can be purchased, along with things like customizable dice, different game boards, and other settings with different rules.

Game features

PLEARN is a virtual board game to raise kids and youths’ awareness about financial literacy. The game has two phases; players vs environment and players vs players.
Plearn Provide many different rewards and incentives that inspire our gamers and increase their performance by completing various missions throughout the game levels. Levels are unlocked based on the player’s current progress. After successful missions completion, the player will be given a new medal, move to the next step in the plearn ranking system, start a new level with new challenges and missions and new awards. The player’s goal in each mission will be to learn new financial aspects.
